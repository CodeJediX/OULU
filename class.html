Why OULU?

Physical Classes

Physical classes offer direct instruction, hands-on learning, and real-time interaction, fostering engagement, accountability, and a strong sense of community.

Online Classes

Live Zoom classes offer an interactive and flexible learning experience from anywhere. Students can engage in real-time discussions, ask questions, and receive instant feedback.

Class Recording

Recorded classes offer flexible learning, allowing students to study anytime, anywhere. Students can pause, rewind, and replay lessons at their own pace.

Study Packs

StudyPacks offer comprehensive learning resources, providing students with access to pre-recorded lectures, tutorials, and materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for classes?

You can register online through our student portal or visit our physical office during working hours. Make sure to bring your student ID and relevant documents.

Can I access recorded lectures?

Yes, all online and physical classes are recorded and uploaded to the Edumix platform. You can access them anytime using your student login credentials.

What are the payment methods available?

We accept online bank transfers, credit/debit cards via our secure gateway, and physical cash payments at our main center.

Is there technical support for the Edumix platform?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated support hotline. If you face any issues connecting to classes or accessing your data-free study packs, our team is ready to help.
