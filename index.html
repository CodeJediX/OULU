FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for OULU Academy?
+
Register online through our student portal or visit our Colombo office during working hours. Bring your student ID and O/L results. Call 072 104 2222 for step-by-step guidance.
Can I access recorded lectures later?
+
Yes — every class is recorded and uploaded to the Edumix platform. Access any lecture at any time using your student login. Dialog SIM users access Edumix completely data-free.
How long does it take to complete the A/L syllabus?
+
OULU Academy's fast-track programme is structured to complete the full A/L syllabus within 12 to 15 months, preparing students to sit the A/L examination confidently on their first attempt.
What payment methods are available?
+
We accept online bank transfers, credit/debit card payments via our secure gateway, and cash payments at our Colombo centre. Flexible monthly instalment options are available on request.
Are classes available for students outside Colombo?
+
Absolutely. Physical classes are held at our Colombo centre and all sessions are simultaneously accessible online via Edumix. Any student anywhere in Sri Lanka can enrol and attend.
Is there technical support for the Edumix platform?
+
Yes. Our dedicated tech support hotline is available to resolve any platform issues quickly so your learning is never interrupted. Contact us on 072 104 2222 for assistance.
